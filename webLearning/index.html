Note...
On my web pages relating to this index I have used a lot of <styles> on my elements coding even though it is not advisable in real life. I wanted to do this to make it easier for me to see what I did to make certain things work, this way when I look at the code I do not always have to keep looking at my 'myStyles.css' file or look at the <styles> </styles> at the top of the current page.

Also the look and feel of these pages are also pretty basic and do not all look very similar as I wanted to keep the code small - to make it easier to look and see how I achieved certain tasks.
(Although I am sure this will change as I add more pages)
